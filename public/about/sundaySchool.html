English Sunday School

Siting inside the church and attending a mass for an hour and a half can be taxing for children. Thus, to make learning about the love of God interesting and fun, volunteers from the church hold separate gatherings for children during each service filled with fun action songs, games and discussions on Bible stories and teachings. Right from toddlers to teenagers (upto 16 years of age), all are part of this dynamic gathering, popularly known as the ‘Sunday School’.


As the Psalmist says ‘Your word I have hidden in my heart, that I might not sin against You’. Learning and reciting Bible verses, especially Psalms and Proverbs, are considered fundamental to the teaching process in Sunday Schools. Apart from the weekly classes, each Sunday school (English, Tamil and Hindi) has its own Christmas Programme and a gift distribution ceremony, where every child gets a Christmas gift.
