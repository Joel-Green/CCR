Choir Masters

All 3 congregations have their respective choirs and all 3 choirs are lead by their ‘Choir masters’. These choir masters, with their vast experience and expertise in music, dedicate their time to teach and flourish the choir members in the art of music. All three choirs have their performances as part of their respective carol services.


Choir Master (English) - Mr. B. Varghese and Ms. Akala
Choir Master (Tamil) - Mr. P. Raja
Choir Master (Hindi) - Mr. Thomas Dawson

Sound Engineers

The Cathedral is an architectural marvel. But the gigantic structure with all its reverberations makes handling sound all the more difficult. Therefore, the Cathedral has a dynamic group of experienced sound engineers that works tirelessly to ensure good sound. All of the equipment at the Cathedral is the best suited for its needs set up under the guidance of Mr. Raja and Mr. Angels.

Mr. Andrew Devasir is the person who handles the sound-mixer and the general sound setup in the church.
